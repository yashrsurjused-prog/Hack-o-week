The Spiritual Beauty of Amritsar

Posted on: February 10, 2026

I finally visited the Golden Temple in Amritsar. It was a dream come true for me. The temple is made of real gold and it looks like it is floating on the water. I spent my evening listening to the prayers and it was the most peaceful moment of my life. I also learned about the history of the Sikh community and how they serve people regardless of their religion. If you go there, do not miss the Kada Prasad!

Climbing the King of Forts: Rajgad

Posted on: January 25, 2026

Rajgad Fort is not just a fort; it is an emotion for people in Maharashtra. I started my trek early in the morning from the Gunjavane village. The climb was quite steep, but once I reached the Padmavati Machi, I forgot all my tiredness. The architecture of the Bale Killa (the highest point) is amazing. Looking at the clouds from such a high altitude made me realize why Shivaji Maharaj chose this as his first capital.

Gujarat: The Land of the World's Tallest Statue

Posted on: January 05, 2026

My recent trip to Gujarat was focused on the Statue of Unity. Standing at 182 meters, this statue of Sardar Vallabhbhai Patel is massive. I took the elevator to the viewing gallery which is located at 153 meters. The view of the Narmada River and the dam is breathtaking. Apart from the statue, the valley of flowers nearby is also very beautiful. It is a great place to learn about the man who united India.
